# Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how HearthVale (墟里) collects, uses, and protects your information.

<br>

## 1.0 Information We Collect
We collect information that helps us provide a safe, reliable, and trustworthy community experience for photography collaborations.

### 1.1 Information you provide to us 
- **Account Information**: When you register, we collect basic details like your phone number, identity authentication (real-name verification), and social profiles.
- **Content**: We store your portfolio images, style tags, collaboration requests, and text communications within the platform to facilitate matching.
- **Trust & Safety Data**: We access credit sources (e.g., Alipay Sesame Credit) with your authorization to display trust badges.

<br>

## 2.0 How we use your information
- **Service Provision**: To display your profile, facilitate matching for photography sessions, and support community interactions.
- **Trust & Safety**: To verify identity and provide credit scoring, ensuring a safe collaboration environment and preventing harassment or fraud.
- **Dispute Resolution**: Chat records and electronic agreements are securely stored to mediate potential disputes and ensure fulfillment of collaboration terms.

<br>

## 3.0 Data Security
We implement strict standard security measures to protect your personal information, facial data, and portfolio content from unauthorized access. Real-name authentication data is encrypted and not publicly displayed.

<br>

## 4.0 Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@hearthvale.com.
